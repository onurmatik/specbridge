# Theme Tokens

## Fonts
- Sans: `Satoshi`
- Display: `Cabinet Grotesk`

## Radius
- Small controls: 8px to 12px
- Standard cards: 16px
- Primary panels: 20px to 28px

## Shadows
- Primary: subtle low-elevation card shadow
- Paper preview: deeper soft shadow for document canvas only

## Surfaces
- App background: `gray-50`
- Primary card: `white`
- Borders: `gray-200`
- Highlight surfaces:
  - Agent: purple-50 / purple-100
  - Decision success: green-50 / green-100
  - Warning: amber-50 / amber-100
  - Danger: red-50 / red-100

## Interaction
- Hover: border darkens slightly, background lightens, shadow increases modestly
- No heavy gradients except very light atmospheric background accents
